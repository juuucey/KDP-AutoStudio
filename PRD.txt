6. Workflow
Step 1: Research & Scoring
‚Ä¢	User enters seed keywords (e.g., ‚Äúgratitude journal‚Äù, ‚Äúkids handwriting workbook‚Äù).
‚Ä¢	Scraper gathers top-selling listings (ASINs, ranks, pricing, reviews).
‚Ä¢	OpenAI summarizes and scores niches; outputs structured backlog.
‚Ä¢	Results are saved in local database and displayed in app.
Step 2: Review Backlog
‚Ä¢	Sort by score, seasonality, or risk.
‚Ä¢	Inspect AI reasoning (‚Äúwhy it sells / why not‚Äù).
‚Ä¢	Approve promising ideas for production.
Step 3: Design with AI
‚Ä¢	App passes approved ideas to Affinity AI:
o	Generate and refine covers/interiors with macros.
o	Import AI-generated content from OpenAI (titles, copy, prompts).
o	Adjust automatically to KDP trim and spine requirements.
Step 4: Validate & Package
‚Ä¢	App runs preflight check (PDF size, bleed, fonts, DPI, spine width).
‚Ä¢	Exports final files into /KDP_AutoStudio/Projects/{bookTitle}/bundle.zip.
Step 5: Track Performance (optional)
‚Ä¢	Input published ASINs; app fetches metrics and updates analytics.
